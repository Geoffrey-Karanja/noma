Our Story

We built NOMA because food deserves better.

NOMA was founded in 2023 by a group of food lovers who were tired of cold deliveries, hidden fees, and apps that felt like they were designed by people who'd never actually ordered food.

We set out to build something different — a platform that treats every order like it matters. Because it does. Whether you're ordering a late-night ramen or a special birthday dinner, NOMA makes sure it arrives perfect.

Today we serve over 15 cities across Africa, connecting thousands of restaurants with millions of hungry customers — and we're just getting started.
